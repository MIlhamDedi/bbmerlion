## Design Context

### Users
BBMerlion serves Indonesian high-school students preparing for Singapore university entrance pathways, especially students looking for practical support around mathematics, physics, tryouts, campus context, and mentor guidance from Indonesian students who have studied in Singapore.

### Brand Personality
Supportive, ambitious, and volunteer-led. The product should feel like a credible senior-student desk: serious enough for exam preparation, warm enough to lower intimidation, and direct enough to help students start studying quickly.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use the recovered BBMerlion archive as the brand base: the red lion mark, midnight-blue navigation, the line "Ya, Kamu bisa!", and the Singapore-university study mission. Modernize it with a clean learning dashboard, strong resource actions, real archive imagery, restrained surfaces, and a light interface with red and teal accents.

### Design Principles
- Put learning actions first: notes, tryouts, and campus guidance should be reachable immediately.
- Preserve the BBMerlion origin story without freezing the UI in the 2018 Bootstrap style.
- Label old information clearly when it may need verification, especially admissions, signup, contact, and social links.
- Use visual assets only when they clarify the study mission or recovered brand history.
- Keep the tone direct, student-friendly, and optimistic without marketing filler.
